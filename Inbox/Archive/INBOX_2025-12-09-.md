Key Findings & Feedback

1. Pricing Sensitivity

* Cost Concern: Pricing is her primary concern. Because she feeds five adults, a significant price hike would make the service prohibitive.

* Vetting Process: Connie validates recipes by watching the video content first. If a creator is disorganized or unclear in a video, she assumes the written recipe will be poor and avoids it.

* Digital Cookbook: She desires a feature to consolidate and save recipes into a "digital cookbook" within the platform.

* Feedback Systems: She distinguishes between two types of communication:

   * Comments: For asking questions before cooking (on the recipe page).

      * She does not think recipe feedback would make sense to be added to the original social media post

   * Ratings: For providing feedback after cooking, which the influencer should use to adjust recipes.

* Curation Tools: She wants the ability to "heart" or follow specific creators and, crucially, a "mute" or "not interested" button to block specific creators and cuisines she dislikes.

* Video Integration: She expects the original video to be linked directly on the HelloFresh recipe page for reference.
