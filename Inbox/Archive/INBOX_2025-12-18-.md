Context

Discount communication presets are predefined fields that allow marketing teams to display discount-related messages or offers on various parts of the website. These presets control the content that appears at key customer touchpoints, located on pages such as Landing Pages, Plans Page, Checkout, Reactivation pages.

Marketing Teams can customize the discount messages based on the field presets e.g. Team can decide to display Free Dessert for Life for some users and Free Snacks for life for others. Each preset field controls specific touchpoints on the site, ensuring a consistent and clear message to customers across the user journey.

Touchpoints itself are owned by different product teams, who manage the placement and configuration of the fields, while marketing handles the content. 

All touchpoints integrated with Communication Presets are listed in documentation.

Governance Process for Adding New Discount Communication Touchpoints

The ownership of discount communication is spread across multiple teams product teams own the touchpoints, while marketing teams handle the benefit communication. This requires teams to be aligned on all available fields. Whenever a new field is introduced, it is critical that the team responsible for that section ensures alignment with marketing.

Notification when new field is added

1. Notification to Marketing & Product Teams via Slack

Each time a team plans to add a new discount communication field, they are required to send a message in a dedicated Slack channel (e.g., #discount-communications-updates). This notification will contain the following information:

Slack Message Template:

* Subject: New Discount Communication Touchpoint Field

* Team: [Insert team name]

* Field Details:

* Planned day of Roll Out: [e.g., 23-Sep]

Most consumers in the US still tend to approach supplement purchases with more flexibility, buying products on an as-needed basis. Nearly half of Gen Z (49%) and more than half of Millennials (53%) buy supplements when required, 58% of Gen X and 57% of Baby Boomers. 



As of today, Factor Form is only available as a subscription. As a result, we are not serving the needs of most of our target customers, or force them into a subscription only for them to cancel later on.

Every product offered on Factor Form will be available for purchase as a subscription as well as a one-time purchase. We will position subscriptions first and offer them at a lower price, as we would like to incentivize customers to stick to the habit in order to see results from the products and stay for subsequent orders. The ability to buy Factor Form products without committing to a subscription or worrying about getting unwanted orders.
