The sessions drop off in PetsTable is significantly lower because of two reasons:





1. The registration page is skipped entirely for customers who logged in a previous session, and are redirected to the checkout page directly by the site cookies. After enabling email autopopulation on the registration page from WKND cookie, the friction on this page has declined further. Therefore, we advocate to keep the registration page, make minimal functional changes and improve the sign up CTA to increase the customer intent further to complete checkout.
