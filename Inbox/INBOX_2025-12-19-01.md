Beam Integration for The Pets Table

	Summary: Beam is a social impact integration platform that enables companies to donate a percentage of a customer's purchase to a charity. Instead of the company choosing the cause, Beam allows the user to select the specific charity they want to support from a curated list. 

The Pets Table plans to launch Beam as a conversion rate improvement tool and retention booster. 

	Created At: Dec 19, 2025

	Last Updated: Dec 19, 2025

	Editor(s): Maria Chelminska

	Context

User Problem

As a pet food subscription user, I am struggling with: 

* Choice Overload: Pet food subscription users are faced with a vast number of choices regarding food types and producers, making it difficult to decide which specific brand to commit to.

* Lack of Purpose: Customers are looking for additional reasons or a sense of purpose when choosing a producer; they often feel "powerless" to make a real difference and want their "consumer power" to result in tangible social impact

Vision

By integrating with Beam, we want to transform the decision to subscribe to The Pets Table into a more emotional experience by providing users with a clear purpose. By allowing customers to choose a high-impact nonprofit to support at no extra cost, the brand shifts the relationship from a standard transaction to a mission-driven partnership

Success Metrics:

* Gross CVR improvement (define the breakeven?) 

* AOR improvement

* CVA improvement

Scope

V1 Focus: For the initial version, the integration will be limited to the conversion funnel.

* Excluded Elements: V1 will not include the active experience component (the post-purchase subscriber dashboard in settings, impact tracking or cancelaltion deflection components).

* Included funnel Touchpoints:

   * Early Funnel Teasers: Informing users about the donation option during the quiz, meal selection, or registration steps.

      * To be decided on which step, candidates: 

         * Loading between funnel and plans selection, 

         * popup message in the quiz (e.g. on the body condition question) - to make it more emotional 

         * registration page, 

   * Charity opt-in and selection on checkout: The primary interaction point where the user selects a curated charity. This component will be available on the /checkout/payment step. For mobile, this will sit below the Order Summary; for web, it will sit between the summary and the assurance message 

  



   * Consider adding a Thank you message after the user interacts with the selection component



   * V2 (if experiment is successful)

   * API integration in order to automate data sharing between TPT and Beam and automate donation transfers.

New Research Approach: We adopted an agile way of working, structuring the 9-week discovery into two-week sprints to enable fast learning and early validation.
